Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Flowcast (“the App”, “we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the App. The App is provided by Umut Demirci, operating as Soulware (“Soulware”), in Türkiye. If you have any questions, contact us at hello@soulware.app.

1. The short version

  • Flowcast is a podcast-native learning app. Your learning paths, progress, saved episodes, saved insights (“parcels”), recaps, and routine settings are stored on your device.
  • The App does not require an account or sign-in.
  • To build your lessons, the goals and challenges you type — and the resulting episode transcripts — are sent through our server to Anthropic (Claude) to generate scripts and to ElevenLabs to synthesize the audio. This content is used only to produce your episodes and is not used to train AI models.
  • We use analytics and crash-reporting services (Mixpanel and Sentry) to keep the App reliable and understand which features help.
  • We do not sell your personal information, and we don’t use it to advertise to you. There is no ad tracking and no cross-app tracking prompt.
  • You can delete your data at any time on your device, and deleting the App removes the data stored on it.

2. Information we collect

Information you provide

The App lets you describe what you want to get better at, choose learning goals, save insights, and record reflections. This may include the goals and challenges you type, your reflection responses, and the paths and episodes you save. You choose what to enter, and this information is stored on your device.

Content you submit to AI features

To generate your personalized episodes, the goals and challenges you type — and the resulting episode transcripts — are sent over a secure connection to our server (a proxy) and on to our AI providers: Anthropic (Claude) to write the episode scripts and ElevenLabs to synthesize the two-host audio. See “How the AI features work” below.

Purchases and subscriptions

Flowcast offers optional paid subscriptions (weekly, monthly, and yearly). Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We receive confirmation of your purchase and subscription status, but we do not receive or store your payment-card details — Apple handles payment under its own terms.

Usage and diagnostic data

To keep the App reliable and improve it, we use analytics and crash-reporting tools — Mixpanel (anonymous product-usage events, such as which features are used) and Sentry (crash and error logs) — that collect limited technical information such as app version, device model, operating-system version, and general usage events. This data is used in aggregate and is not used to identify you personally.

What we do not collect

We do not access your Contacts, Photos, Microphone, or precise location. We do not sell your information, and we do not use your data to serve advertising. We do not track you across other companies’ apps and websites, so the App does not present an App Tracking Transparency prompt.

3. How we use information

  • To provide the App’s core features — your learning paths, generated audio episodes, reflection checkpoints, saved insights, episode chat, recaps, and routine reminders.
  • To generate the scripts and audio for your personalized episodes.
  • To process and manage your subscription status.
  • To keep the App secure, fix bugs, understand which features are useful, and improve performance.
  • To respond to your questions and support requests.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Where your data is stored

Your learning paths, progress, saved episodes, saved insights, recaps, and routine settings are stored locally on your device. We do not operate user accounts or a central database that holds your personal learning content. Certain content is transmitted to the third-party service providers described below in order to deliver specific features (AI script generation, audio synthesis, purchases, and analytics/crash reporting). If a future version of the App adds optional cloud sync or accounts, this policy will be updated before that feature is released.

5. When we share information

We share information only with the service providers we rely on to run the App, and only as needed. We do not sell your personal information. Our service providers include:

  • Anthropic (Claude) — processes the goals/challenges you type and episode context to generate the episode scripts.
  • ElevenLabs — processes the generated scripts/transcripts to synthesize the two-host audio.
  • Mixpanel and Sentry — receive limited, anonymous diagnostic and usage data to help us keep the App stable and useful.
  • Apple — processes and manages purchases and subscriptions and delivers notifications.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to enforce our Terms, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users or the public.

6. How the AI features work

When you ask Flowcast to build a path or generate an episode, the content needed to fulfil your request — the goals and challenges you type, and the resulting episode transcripts — is sent over a secure connection through our server to Anthropic (Claude) to write the script and to ElevenLabs to synthesize the audio. We send only what is needed for the feature. This content is used only to produce your episodes and is not used to train AI models; it is handled subject to each provider’s terms and data-protection commitments. Flowcast’s audio lessons are AI-generated educational content for general information only — they may be inaccurate or imperfect, and they are not professional, medical, financial, legal, or mental-health advice.

7. App permissions

  • Notifications — requested so the App can send the routine and reflection reminders you set up.

We do not request access to your Contacts, location, microphone, or photos. You can change or revoke the Notifications permission at any time in your device’s Settings.

8. Data retention and deletion

Your on-device data remains until you delete it. You can delete your learning paths, saved episodes, saved insights, recaps, and history on your device, and deleting the App removes the data stored on it. Diagnostic and analytics data is retained for a limited period in aggregate form. Content sent to our AI providers is handled under their terms and is not used to train their models. To ask us about data we may hold, email hello@soulware.app.

9. Your privacy rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights over your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict its use, to object to certain processing, and to data portability.

  • EEA / UK (GDPR): you may exercise the rights above and lodge a complaint with your local data-protection authority. Because most data stays on your device, you control much of it directly within the App.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): you have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion, and to not be discriminated against for exercising your rights. We do not sell or “share” personal information as those terms are defined under California law.

To make a request, contact hello@soulware.app. We may need to verify your request.

10. Children’s privacy

The App is intended for a general audience and is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

11. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including secure connections for data sent to our service providers. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

12. International data transfers

Our service providers (such as the AI, analytics, and purchase providers) may process data in countries outside your own, including the United States. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

13. Tracking & advertising

We do not track you across other companies’ apps and websites for advertising purposes, we do not use an advertising identifier, and we do not serve ads. Because we do not track you in this way, the App does not present an App Tracking Transparency prompt.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice. Your continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.

15. Contact us

Questions or requests about your privacy? Email hello@soulware.app.
