Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

This is a starting template. Review it with the people you trust before publishing, and make sure it reflects exactly how the app handles data.

Ivy Relations Assistant ("we", "us", "the app") helps you remember and nurture your personal relationships. We built it to be private by design. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have.

Information you provide

  • People & moments. Names, dates (birthdays, anniversaries, milestones), notes, and goals you choose to add.
  • Account details. If you create an account, basic information needed to sign in and sync.

How we use it

  • To show your timeline, send the reminders you set up, and generate message and gift suggestions.
  • To provide and improve the app's features and reliability.

AI features

When you ask Ivy Relations Assistant to suggest a message, gift, or plan, the relevant details may be processed to generate a response. We do not sell your data, and we do not use the contents of your relationships to advertise to you.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers that help us run the app, and only as needed to provide it to you.

Your choices

  • You can edit or delete people, moments, and goals at any time.
  • You can request deletion of your account and associated data.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@soulware.app.
